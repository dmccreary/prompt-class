# AI and Machine Learning Foundations

## Summary

This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts of artificial intelligence and machine learning that form the bedrock of modern prompt engineering. Students will learn how large language models work, from neural networks and transformer architectures to tokenization and inference. Understanding these foundations is essential for crafting effective prompts, as it demystifies how AI systems process and generate language. By the end of this chapter, students will have a solid mental model of what happens behind the scenes when they interact with an AI chatbot.
