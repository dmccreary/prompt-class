# Prompt Fundamentals

## Summary

This chapter covers the essential principles of writing effective prompts for large language models. Students will learn how to structure prompts with clarity and specificity, avoid ambiguity, and decompose complex tasks into manageable instructions. The chapter also introduces the iterative process of prompt testing and evaluation, teaching students to assess response quality across dimensions of relevance, accuracy, completeness, and conciseness.
