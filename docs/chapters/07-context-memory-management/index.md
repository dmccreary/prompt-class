# Context, Memory, and Information Management

## Summary

This chapter addresses one of the most critical challenges in prompt engineering: managing context effectively within the constraints of AI model context windows. Students will learn techniques for prompt compression and expansion, token counting, and relevance filtering to make the most of limited context space. The chapter covers conversation history management, session persistence, and strategies for working with long-form documents. Students will also master information extraction, summarization, and domain knowledge priming to build rich, contextually aware prompts.
