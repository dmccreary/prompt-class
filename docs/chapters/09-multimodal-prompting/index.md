# Multimodal Prompting

## Summary

This chapter expands prompt engineering beyond text to encompass images, documents, audio, and video. Students will learn how to craft effective prompts for multimodal AI systems, including image description, visual question answering, document analysis, and chart reading. The chapter covers practical skills such as PDF processing, screenshot analysis, and generating alt text for accessibility. Students will also explore image generation prompts and data visualization techniques that leverage multimodal capabilities.
