# Ethics and Responsible AI

## Summary

This chapter addresses the critical ethical dimensions of working with AI systems. Students will examine bias, fairness, transparency, and accountability in AI-generated content, developing a framework for responsible use. The chapter covers privacy considerations, consent and data use, intellectual property, and copyright issues that arise when using AI tools. Students will also learn to recognize and mitigate misinformation risks and understand the implications of deepfake technology, building the ethical awareness necessary for responsible prompt engineering practice.
