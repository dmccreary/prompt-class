# Evaluation and Optimization

## Summary

This chapter provides systematic approaches to measuring, testing, and improving the performance of both prompts and AI agents. Students will learn evaluation methodologies including A/B testing, benchmark testing, and quality rubric design for both human and automated assessment. The chapter covers agent-specific evaluation concerns such as reliability, error recovery, and retry logic. Students will also explore practical optimization strategies including token efficiency, batch processing, model selection, and performance monitoring to build robust, production-ready AI systems.
