# Usage Limits and Token Economics

## Summary

This chapter demystifies the economic and operational aspects of working with AI models at scale. Students will learn how API pricing models work, how to estimate and monitor token costs, and how to manage rate limits and usage quotas effectively. The chapter covers practical concerns such as the five-hour token window, throttling, cooldown periods, and plan tier differences. Students will develop strategies for cost-effective prompting, budget management, and understanding the environmental impact of AI usage, essential knowledge for any production deployment.
