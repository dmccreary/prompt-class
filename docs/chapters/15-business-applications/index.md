# Business Applications

## Summary

This chapter bridges prompt engineering theory and real-world business practice, demonstrating how to apply AI effectively across a wide range of professional contexts. Students will learn to build prompts for customer service, content marketing, email drafting, meeting summarization, and report automation. The chapter covers strategic applications including market research, competitive analysis, and decision support, as well as creative tasks such as brainstorming facilitation and social media content creation. Students will develop a practical toolkit for integrating AI into everyday business workflows.
