# Capstone Projects

## Summary

This final chapter brings together every skill and concept from the entire course into comprehensive, real-world capstone projects. Students will design and build complete AI-powered systems spanning customer support bots, content pipelines, research assistants, and code review tools. The chapter includes projects across business, education, legal, and technical domains, from resume analyzers and brand voice systems to RAG pipeline builders and multi-agent task runners. Each project challenges students to integrate prompt engineering, security, ethics, evaluation, and agentic AI into polished, production-quality solutions.
