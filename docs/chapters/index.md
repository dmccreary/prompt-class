# Prompt Engineering: Intelligent Textbook

Welcome to the Prompt Engineering Intelligent Textbook. This course provides a comprehensive journey through the art and science of communicating effectively with large language models. From foundational AI concepts through advanced agentic systems and real-world applications, each chapter builds on the previous ones to develop your prompt engineering expertise.

## Chapters

1. [AI and Machine Learning Foundations](./01-ai-ml-foundations/index.md) - Understand the core AI and machine learning concepts that underpin modern language models.
2. [Prompt Fundamentals](./02-prompt-fundamentals/index.md) - Learn the essential principles of writing clear, effective prompts.
3. [Prompt Types and Model Parameters](./03-prompt-types-parameters/index.md) - Explore different prompt categories and the model settings that influence output.
4. [Core Prompt Techniques](./04-core-prompt-techniques/index.md) - Master foundational techniques like zero-shot, few-shot, and chain-of-thought prompting.
5. [Advanced Prompt Techniques](./05-advanced-prompt-techniques/index.md) - Apply sophisticated strategies including role assignment, persona prompting, and meta-prompting.
6. [Output Format Control](./06-output-format-control/index.md) - Direct AI models to produce output in specific formats such as JSON, Markdown, tables, and more.
7. [Context, Memory, and Information Management](./07-context-memory-management/index.md) - Manage context windows, conversation history, and information extraction effectively.
8. [Retrieval-Augmented Generation](./08-retrieval-augmented-generation/index.md) - Combine language models with external knowledge sources for grounded, accurate responses.
9. [Multimodal Prompting](./09-multimodal-prompting/index.md) - Work with images, documents, audio, and video alongside text-based prompts.
10. [Ethics and Responsible AI](./10-ethics-responsible-ai/index.md) - Navigate the ethical considerations of AI use including bias, fairness, and intellectual property.
11. [Prompt Security](./11-prompt-security/index.md) - Defend against prompt injection, jailbreaking, and other adversarial attacks.
12. [Agentic AI](./12-agentic-ai/index.md) - Build and orchestrate AI agents that use tools, plan actions, and execute multi-step workflows.
13. [Evaluation and Optimization](./13-evaluation-optimization/index.md) - Measure, test, and improve prompt and agent performance systematically.
14. [Usage Limits and Token Economics](./14-usage-limits-token-economics/index.md) - Understand token costs, rate limits, and strategies for cost-effective AI usage.
15. [Business Applications](./15-business-applications/index.md) - Apply prompt engineering to real-world business tasks from customer service to strategic analysis.
16. [Educational Applications](./16-educational-applications/index.md) - Leverage AI for teaching, tutoring, assessment, and curriculum development.
17. [Capstone Projects](./17-capstone-projects/index.md) - Synthesize all skills into comprehensive, real-world prompt engineering projects.
