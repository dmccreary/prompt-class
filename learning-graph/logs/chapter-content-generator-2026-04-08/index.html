    Style Guide Compliance

    All chapters follow the Content Generation Style Guide from CLAUDE.md:

    • Fun, upbeat, positive tone throughout
    • "Superpower" framing present
    • Gentle mockery of hype culture ("10 Secret Prompts" genre)
    • Quiet productivity wins celebrated (people looking smarter at work)
    • Polly the Parrot used appropriately with varied admonition types
    • Terms defined before appearing in tables/diagrams (scaffolding)
    • No more than 3 paragraphs without a non-text element
    • Key Takeaways sections with bullet points in every chapter
    • Before/after prompt examples where appropriate
